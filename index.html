I loved being able to help people through the medium of technology, and I wanted to be able to help once again. I had gotten certified in SQL in February of 2021, and Java shortly after, in hopes of being a little more helpful in an ecommerce setting. At the current moment, I am working toward a UI Designer certification.

I am a passionate, driven, and dedicated individual who always seeks to learn more about the web in general. I thrive in environments where I can share ideas with teammates, develop big ideas from small sketches, and keep clients up to date with the latest information in regards to their project.
